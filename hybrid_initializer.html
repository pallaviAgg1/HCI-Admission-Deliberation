Study primer: what you will do

You will review twelve synthetic applicants and make an admit or reject decision for each one. Please use the profile details, statement, and your own judgment to decide how you would evaluate them.

The system will then summarize your overall admissions stance, ask a few follow-up questions, and estimate whether your decisions change in the race-sensitivity simulation.

For this study, race is the only socially contested attribute being examined. The simulation keeps every other applicant detail fixed and checks whether changing race alone would shift your decisions or scores.

This is a reflection tool, not a policy test. There are no right answers, but we do ask that you complete all twelve decisions before moving on.
